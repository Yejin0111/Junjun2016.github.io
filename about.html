Biography

I am a Young Scientist at Shanghai AI Laboratory (上海人工智能实验室) and a Full-time Advisor at Shanghai Academy of Creative Intelligence (上海创智学院). I lead the General Medical AI (GMAI) team at Shanghai AI Laboratory, focusing on building and open-sourcing large-scale medical benchmarks and foundation models for the medical AI community.

Our GMAI team has built and open-sourced multiple largest-scale medical benchmarks and best-performing models in medical AI, with representative works including: the largest 3D medical image pre-trained model STU-Net, the largest medical segmentation foundation models SAM-Med2D and SAM-Med3D, the most comprehensive medical multimodal evaluation benchmarks OmniMedVQA and GMAI-MMBench, the general medical multimodal large model GMAI-VL, the ultra-high-resolution pathology WSI multimodal large model SlideChat, and the largest surgical multimodal models OphCLIP and Ophora. Our recent open-source initiative Project Imaging-X, a large-scale medical imaging data survey and platform, has attracted wide attention.

I have won 10+ awards in international competitions, including 6 championships. Our paper was shortlisted for the MICCAI 2025 Best Paper and Young Scientist Awards and received the MICCAI 2025 Best Workshop Paper Award.

I received my Ph.D. degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU) in 2021, under the supervision of Prof. Lixu Gu. During my Ph.D., I was fortunate to work with Prof. Yu Qiao at Multimedia Lab (MMLab), Shenzhen Institute of Advanced Technology (SIAT), Chinese Academy of Sciences.
